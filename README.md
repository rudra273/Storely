# Storely

**Storely is a simple, offline-first inventory and billing app for shop owners.**
Set up your shop, add stock, bill customers, track who owes you money, and share
professional GST invoices — all from your phone, even without an internet
connection.

Available on the Google Play Store for Android.

---

## What Storely is for

Running a small store means juggling stock, prices, bills, and customers — often
on paper or in your head. Storely puts all of it in one place and keeps it on
your phone. You can run your whole day without internet, and optionally connect
the cloud later when you want your data backed up or shared with your staff.

It's built for a practical, real-world workflow: set up the shop once, add stock
as you buy it, bill customers as they walk in, and keep an eye on what's running
low and who hasn't paid.

---

## What you can do

### Products & stock
- Keep a full product list with name, code, barcode, price, unit, and category.
- Add stock through **purchases** — record the date, supplier, quantity, and cost.
- Let Storely calculate selling price automatically from GST, overhead, and
  profit margin, or just type the price directly.
- Import your existing products in bulk from a CSV or Excel file.
- See low-stock items at a glance so you know what to reorder.

### Billing
- Create bills in seconds — scan a barcode or pick products manually.
- Apply discounts, choose a payment method, and mark bills paid, partly paid, or
  unpaid.
- Storely handles GST for you (CGST / SGST / IGST) and keeps a snapshot on each
  bill so your records stay correct even if prices change later.
- **Customise your invoices** — choose your invoice title, which columns and
  details appear, add your shop logo and a digital signature.
- **Print or share PDF bills** directly to WhatsApp, email, or any app.
- Cancel a bill when needed and Storely automatically puts the stock back and
  fixes the customer's balance.

### Customers & suppliers
- Keep a contact list of your customers and suppliers.
- Track each customer's total purchases and outstanding (unpaid) balance.
- Store GST details for business (B2B) customers.

### Insights
- A clear analytics view of your sales, profit, unpaid bills, and top products.
- Quickly find unpaid bills so you can follow up on payments.

### Labels & scanning
- Generate **QR or barcode label sheets** for your products to print and stick on
  shelves or items.
- Scan those labels with your phone camera to bill faster.

## How your data works

Storely is **offline-first**. By default, everything you enter — products,
bills, customers, settings — is stored **on your device**. There's no account to
create and nothing leaves your phone unless you choose to enable cloud sync.

**Optional cloud sync** lets you back up your data and access it across devices,
and lets your staff work from their own phones. Cloud sync is entirely your
choice and stays off until you turn it on.

Your privacy and data handling are explained in full inside the app under
**Privacy Policy**.

## Getting started in the app

1. Complete your **Shop Profile** and GST status.
2. Set your **Pricing Defaults** and **Bill Settings**.
3. Add suppliers, categories, and units as needed.
4. Add stock through **Products → New Purchase**.
5. Start billing from **Scan & Bill** or **Manual Bill**.
